HIGH-LEVEL GAME
---------------

Udder Destruction is a top-down survivor-style action game starring Moolissa
Hoofman. The player moves with WASD or arrow keys while powers fire
automatically. Defeated enemies award Bovinity and may drop pickups. Filling
the Bovinity bar pauses the game and offers three randomly selected powers.

The player begins with Stomp level 1. Powers can reach level 10.

The current arena is a finite 100 x 100 grass-tile playing field surrounded by
a 24-tile-deep pond border. Normal enemies and bosses avoid water. The player
may enter water, but moves at half speed and takes 15% of maximum HP in
drowning damage every second.


CORE PLAYER BEHAVIOR
--------------------

Implemented in:
- Assets/UdderDestruction/Scripts/UdderPlayer.cs
- Assets/UdderDestruction/Prefabs/Moolissa Player.prefab

Current base values:
- Max HP: 100
- Move speed: 4.8
- Projectile damage: 6
- Base Stomp damage: 5.5
- Base Cheese It chance: 1%
- Maximum power level: 10
- Starting power: Stomp level 1
- Starting Bovinity requirement: 5
- Later Bovinity requirement: 5 * current Bovinity level

Behavior:
- The cow faces its current movement direction or the last direction moved.
- Attacks do not fire unless a valid target is within that attack's range.
- Milk regenerates internally, but the old milk HUD bar has been removed.
- On reaching 0 HP, Cheese It is rolled.
- Successful Cheese It restores full HP, grants 2.2 seconds of invulnerability,
  and displays "Cheesed it!" above the cow for 3 seconds.
- If Cheese It fails, the game-over sequence runs. Restart is not implemented.

Game-over text sequence:
1. "Don't cry" fades over 1 second.
2. "Over" remains and reveals a wooden Main Menu button at the bottom of the
   panel.
3. "Spilled Milk" fades over 1 second.
4. "Game" appears above "Over" and remains.
- The Main Menu button reloads the active scene into its clean main-menu state.


POWERS
------

ACTIVE / AUTOMATIC POWERS

Stomp
- Contact damage when an enemy touches the cow.
- Starts at level 1.
- Each level adds Stomp damage.
- Stomp kills use the cotton-then-skull death sequence.

Whole Milk
- Fast auto-aimed projectile.
- Base firing interval: 0.38 seconds.
- Whole Milk sprite is rotated 180 degrees when fired.
- Its damage multiplier is currently 0.5 before level scaling.

Buttermilk
- Auto-aimed milk projectile.
- Base firing interval: 1 second.
- Makes the target slip in its current/last movement direction.
- Base slip duration is 1 second, +20% per level after level 1.
- Buttermilk slips do not count toward the Butter Chicken achievement.

Spoiled Milk
- One projectile every 5 seconds.
- Preferentially targets a valid dolphin when one is in range.
- On ordinary impact, creates a repulsing puddle lasting 5 seconds.
- Puddles use the slime Idle, Hurt, and Death animations.
- Puddles cause no damage.
- Enemies avoid puddles and attempt to leave them immediately.
- Hitting a dolphin beaches it and pollutes the nearby pond tile.
- Polluted pond tiles turn green and cannot spawn dolphins until the next wave.

Raw Milk
- Auto-aimed milk projectile.
- Base firing interval: 1 second.
- Applies the "Diseased!" damage-over-time status.
- Diseased normal enemies can spread Raw Milk disease by contact.
- Bosses take Raw Milk damage and DoT but are not held or slowed by it.

Butter
- Drops a single scalable butter slick every 5.5 seconds.
- Uses Assets/Farming Asset Pack/Butter_Puddle.png.
- Odd levels increase duration.
- Even levels increase the size of the single slick.
- Current world size formula reaches 2.0 x 2.0 at level 10.
- Current base duration is 8 seconds, +2 seconds per odd upgrade.
- Butter causes enemies to slide and displays "Whoopsie!".

Dairy Air
- Creates a cloud every 8.5 seconds.
- Enemies touched by it become lactose intolerant for the rest of their life.
- Lactose-intolerant enemies take a 1.65 dairy damage multiplier.
- Leveling extends cloud life toward 200% uptime.

Prion Infection
- Fires every 6 seconds at the nearest valid non-boss target within 6.5 units.
- It is now a homing projectile using 540_Platinum_Gear.
- Projectile prefab:
  Assets/UdderDestruction/Prefabs/Prion Infection Gear.prefab
- Current prefab tuning:
  speed 5.5, life 2 seconds, spin 240 degrees/second, homing strength 7.
- The gear rotates around its centered pivot while flying.
- It ignores dolphins, sea urchins, bosses, and unrelated enemies.
- Impact applies persistent Prion Infection.
- Infected enemies take DoT and attack other enemies, including infected ones.
- Infected enemies display the 21_Very angry sprite over their heads.
- On infected enemy death, nearby eligible enemies each roll for infection.
- Spread AoE radius equals the dead enemy's collider diameter.
- Spread chance is 10% at level 1, +1 percentage point per additional level.
- Prion DoT is 1 damage/second at level 1, +0.25 per additional level.


PASSIVE POWERS

Condensed Milk
- Whole Milk, Buttermilk, and Raw Milk gain damage over time.
- Duration: 0.3 seconds per level.
- Total DoT damage: 10% of the source attack damage per level.

I Like to MOOve It
- +10% base movement speed per level.

Beefcake
- +10% current Stomp damage per level.
- +10% base maximum HP per level.

Legen-dairy!
- +1 percentage point to Cheese It chance per level.

More Cowbell
- +10% enemies per wave per level.
- +10% drop chance multiplier per level.

Maillard Reaction
- Regenerates 10% HP periodically.
- Interval improves from 3 seconds toward 1 second by level 10.

Rawhide
- Level 1 grants 10% damage resistance.
- Each later level adds 1 percentage point.

Aura Farming
- No longer has a visual and does not damage enemies.
- Attracts nearby pickups.
- Attraction radius equals player collider radius * power level.
- Once a pickup enters the attraction radius, it continues following the
  player until collected even if it later leaves the radius.

ENEMIES AND WAVES
-----------------

Normal enemies:
- Debt Chicken
- Hostile Ham
- Enemy cow escorts
- Dolphins and sea urchins are special enemies/hazards.

Normal wave behavior:
- Wave enemy count: ceil((4 + wave * 2) * More Cowbell multiplier).
- Wave 1 contains chickens only.
- Pigs begin at wave 5 with a 10% composition ratio.
- Waves 11-20 use 20% pigs.
- Pig ratio rises by 10 percentage points each decade, capped at 50%.
- Spawn interval starts at 1.65 seconds and improves by 0.055 seconds per wave,
  with a minimum of 0.32 seconds.
- Enemy speed scales from 0.78 to 1.28 through approximately wave 25.
- Normal enemies and bosses avoid the pond border.
- Enemies use light separation so they do not completely overlap.

Normal enemy prefab colliders:
- Debt Chicken: CircleCollider2D radius 0.12, offset (0, 0).
- Hostile Ham: CircleCollider2D radius 0.13, offset (0, -0.02).
- Miyamoto Moosashi/base boss prefab: radius 0.166.

Death visuals:
- Normal enemies, bosses, and dolphins use cotton followed by skull.
- "KO" text is no longer used.

Drop roll after a normal enemy death:
- Minor MOOna: cumulative roll below 20%.
- Normal MOOna: next 9%.
- Remarkable MOOna: next 4%.
- Elysian MOOna: next 1%.
- Cranberry: next 3.5%.
- Strawberry: next 2.5%.
- Raspberry: next 2%.
- Blackberries: next 2%.
- Dairy Double: next 3%.
- More Cowbell increases drop chance through the current roll multiplier.


BOSSES
------

Boss waves occur every 10 waves.

- Wave 10 always uses Miyamoto Moosashi.
- Later boss waves randomly select from the current boss pool.
- The first encounter with a boss is boss-only, except inherent escorts.
- Repeat encounters include normal enemies equal to the current wave number.
  Those reinforcements use normal spawn timing instead of appearing together.
- Bosses avoid ponds.
- Bosses are immune to Prion Infection and status control effects.
- Bosses can still slip on butter and take damage over time.
- Bosses have a simple red health bar and black name text above them.
- Boss-name text is currently 2.5 times larger than its previous size.
- Defeating a boss displays animated "AU LAIT!" for 5 seconds with alternating
  red and yellow letters.

Current boss pool:

Miyamoto Moosashi
- Always fought at wave 10.
- Standard current boss behavior.

Lidia
- Charges toward the player in a straight line.
- Charge currently has a 0.45-second windup, 0.8-second duration, and 3.6-second
  cooldown. Charge contact damage is strongly increased.

Bob Moorley
- Spawns with three chicken escorts.

Hugh Hoofner
- Spawns with five cow escorts.

The Holy Cow
- Cannot be attacked.
- Fully heals the player, fills the Bovinity bar, then leaves.

The Ruminator
- On first death says "I'll be back".
- Revives once with double HP and half speed.

BEEatrice
- Uses the 21_Wasp sprite and is not a cow.
- Spawns with 12 bee drones using the 22_Bee_Drone sprite.
- Drones are one-third BEEatrice's height and orbit her while guarding.
- Three drones at a time attack the player, rotating attack duty every second.
- BEEatrice is completely immune and excluded from auto-aim until every drone
  is dead.
- Defeating BEEatrice drops a honeycomb using 30_Honeycomb.
- Honeycomb grants +10 Bovinity, heals 40% HP, and attracts every unclaimed
  pickup on the field to the player.


PONDS, DOLPHINS, AND DROWNING
-----------------------------

Implemented in:
- Assets/UdderDestruction/Scripts/UdderWorldStreamer.cs
- Assets/UdderDestruction/Scripts/UdderGameController.cs
- Assets/UdderDestruction/Scripts/UdderDolphinSurface.cs

- The arena has no interior ponds.
- Pond tiles form a continuous 24-tile-deep border around the grass arena.
- Pond tiles use trigger BoxCollider2D components for water registration.
- Enemies and bosses strictly avoid water.
- The player can enter water but is slowed by 50%.
- The player takes 15% max HP drowning damage every second.
- Sky-blue "YOU'RE DROWNING!" text appears while drowning.
- Dolphins spawn only on visible, unpolluted pond tiles within 5 units of the
  player.
- Dolphins throw sea urchins only when the player is within 5 units.
- Dolphins only take direct damage from Stomp.
- Spoiled Milk beaches a dolphin onto an adjacent grass tile.
- Beached dolphins stop firing and take dehydration damage until they die.
- Polluted water resets at the beginning of each wave.

PROJECT-SPECIFIC DEVELOPMENT RULES
----------------------------------

These rules were explicitly established during development:

1. Prefer editable scene GameObjects and prefabs.
   Do not create things procedurally in code unless they truly must be runtime
   objects. The user needs to manipulate UI, colliders, enemies, terrain tiles,
   powers, and other visual/gameplay objects in Unity.

2. Respect manual Unity edits.
   The user manually repositions, resizes, recolors, and adjusts colliders and
   UI. Do not overwrite those values at runtime or rebuild them casually.

3. Unity editor blocking rule.
   If the Unity editor is open and blocking file/asset operations, stop and
   tell the user. The user will confirm after closing it, then retry.

4. Do not revert unrelated work.
   The working tree may contain user changes and previous uncommitted work.
   Never reset, revert, or overwrite unrelated changes.

5. UI style.
   Use Fantasy Wooden GUI Free for normal UI elements.
   Use BMYEONSUNG_TMP for normal UI text.
   Keep PixelBattleText for combat/battle text.

6. Combat callout restraint.
   Do not display generic power-name callouts during combat. Use meaningful
   status/event callouts only.

7. Prefab-first tuning.
   Runtime code should respect prefab values instead of overwriting editable
   values such as scale, collider, speed, lifetime, and visuals.

8. Main menu and HUD must remain editable Canvas objects.
   Do not replace them with procedural-only equivalents.

2. Use a narrow Unity batch-mode builder only when asset creation/assignment is
   required.

4. Inspect the resulting prefab and SampleScene references.
5. Playtest in Unity for visual/physics behavior.
